Opening Word

Summer in Wexford is very much intertwined with exhibitions. Living in Gorey, I have always enjoyed our August bank holiday festival. The town comes alive with festivities and many dozens of temporary galleries. This year, of course, has halted the festival which has dissapointed many. However when repressed like such, we should strive to innovate and overcome. The idea of a Virtual Gallery was born from this, a platform for artists to display their works, to share their minds, in a place accessible by all: the Internet. The following works in this exhibition are made by artists of all types and backgrounds, connected by their place of Wexford.

• Curator and Webmaster - Evan Kenny
