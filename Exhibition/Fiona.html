Fiona Somers

Fiona's favourite thing about painting is the lack of boundaries and fear, the impermanence and ability to change your work freely. She also enjoys how gestural, expressive and graphic she is able to be in her work. The history and versatility painting offers is what she would like to explore and experiment with going forward.
