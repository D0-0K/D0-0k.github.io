Warren Callaghan

"Who am you"-Acrylic on Canvas.
"Spring time"-Acrylic on Canvas.
Unnamed - Tape Sculpture.

In his work, Warren tries to capture the human psyche and endless mind. His paintings are inspired by different cultures. While he paints he thinks of their history and tries best to respect it. Warren wants to teach art as a possitive outlet for people with learning dissablities and at risk youths.
