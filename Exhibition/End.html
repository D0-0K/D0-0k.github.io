Closing Word

This year, we have seen many things come to a stop. Work, school, normal life. However all through this time, art has never stopped. Through war and disease, and many oppositions, art has never wavered. So to those reading this, if you feel inspiration after this, take it and create! The only thing stopping us is that we havent started yet. Many thanks to every who has taken part in this exhibition, as well as everyone who has support this vision. And most of all,

Thank you.
